The problem

Yerevan ran out of room — at ground level.

Courtyards are full. Curbsides are double-parked. Every new building competes for the same scarce ground. Vertical parking changes the math: instead of spreading cars across the city, it stacks them on the footprint you already have.

Who it's for

One system. Three kinds of space problem.

Whether you're breaking ground on a tower or squeezing a second car into a driveway, the principle is the same — go vertical.

For developers & construction

Design it in from the first drawing

We join your project at the design stage and integrate the parking structure into the building plan. By handover, parking is built and ready — without surrendering sellable floor area to ramps and bays. From the start of construction, the vertical system grows with the building.

Business centres, offices, retail and hotels get far more parking on the same land — turning a crowded lot into enough space for staff, tenants and visitors. Add optional per-slot EV charging and remote management, and start with a single pilot unit before scaling across a portfolio.

Built for here

Engineered for Armenian conditions.

We specify systems around the things that actually matter in Yerevan — earthquakes, power cuts, harsh seasons, and a growing number of EVs.

Seismic-ready

Systems specified for seismic zones, with structural reinforcement and emergency locking triggered by ground movement.

Never trapped

Manual override and fail-safe locking let you retrieve your car during a complete power outage — no car held hostage.

EV-charging ready

Optional Type 2 / CCS charging per slot, with smart load balancing and remote, per-slot monitoring.

All-weather

Corrosion-resistant finishes and outdoor-rated builds for Yerevan's hot summers and cold, snowy winters.

Small footprint

Reclaim ground space and fit more vehicles into less land — the core advantage of going vertical.

Long service life

Built for years of daily cycles, with a clear maintenance schedule and parts support behind every unit.
